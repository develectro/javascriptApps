Health & Wellness Calculator

Healthy Weight Calculator

Calculates a general healthy weight range for adults (18+ years) based on the Body Mass Index (BMI). For children and adolescents, BMI interpretation requires age and gender-specific percentile charts; consult a pediatrician.

Sleep Calculator

Calculates recommended sleep duration based on age and suggests a bedtime if you provide a desired wake-up time.

Blood Pressure Range Calculator

Provides general information on typical blood pressure ranges by age. This is not medical advice.

Disclaimer: These are general guidelines. Blood pressure can vary based on many factors. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice, diagnosis, and accurate assessments.

Daily Walk Recommendation

Provides general daily walking recommendations based on age, including an approximate distance in kilometers (assuming an average step length of 0.762 meters). These are not strict medical guidelines.

Disclaimer: These are general activity suggestions. Consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new exercise program, especially if you have underlying health conditions.
